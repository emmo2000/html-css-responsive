Fun Facts!


  1. Lightning McQueen's real name is Montgomery McQueen, and he was named after Glenn McQueen, a supervising animator at Pixar Studios.

  2. Lightning McQueen is a mix between a stock car and a Le Mans endurance racer.

  3. In the original Cars movie, Lightning McQueen is in his early-to-mid twenties.

  4. Lightning McQueen was the Piston Cup Rookie of the Year in 2005 and was inducted into the Piston Cup Hall of Fame in 2019.
